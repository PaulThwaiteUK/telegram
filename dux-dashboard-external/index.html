Digital UX Team - Product Readiness Dashboard

The Digital User Experience (DUX) team is building new digital products using data from university systems Pure and Subscribe.
The purpose of this dashboard is to provide an overall readiness report on staff profile and research projects.

Staff profiles

The new staff profile is driven by 25 data fields from Pure and Subscribe. Faculties can use this dashboard to help staff complete their new staff profile.
For guidance on how to update the new staff profile, see our dedicated staff profile guidance Sharepoint site.
Select a dashboard:
  • Staff profile faculty progress - staff profile readiness by faculty and school
  • Staff profile overall progress - graph illustration of the overall progress of staff profiles across the university since the beta launch on 2 December 2021
  • Staff profile engagement - graph illustration of overall staff profile engagement for eligible staff

Research projects

A research project is driven by various data fields from Pure. Faculties can use this dashboard to help staff complete their research project.
For guidance on how to update a research project on Pure, see our dedicated research project guidance Sharepoint site.
Select a dashboard:
  • Research projects faculty progress - research projects readiness by faculty and school
  • Research projects NIHR funder analysis - a list of research projects by NIHR funder
