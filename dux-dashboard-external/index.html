Digital UX - Product readiness dashboard

The Digital User Experience (DUX) team is building new digital products using data from university systems Pure and Subscribe.

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide an overall readiness report on staff profile (and soon research projects).

Staff profiles

The new staff profile is driven by 25 data fields from Pure and Subscribe. Faculties can use this dashboard to help staff complete their staff profile.

For guidance on how to update the new staff profile, see our dedicated staff profile guidance sharepoint site.

For help with the dashboard, use the weekly clinic (Wednesday, 10:15am) with the DUX team in the first instance. This will enable other faculties to benefit from your query.
