The Digital User Experience (DUX) team is building a suite of new digital products. A subset of these products are now in private beta across the university and are accompanied by a dedicated Sharepoint guidance site.
Bugs are welcome for theses products:
  • Staff profile product
  • Staff profile guidance Sharepoint site
  • Research projects product
  • Research project guidance Sharepoint site
Before reporting a bug, please review known problems with staff profiles in case it has already been raised.
Complete all sections of the form including your name and email address.
Please report one bug at a time.

What to expect after you report a bug

Once we have recieved your bug report:
  • Bugs will be assessed by our team. Once they have been confirmed, they will be added to the known problems with staff profiles section of the staff profile guidance Sharepoint site.
  • Bugs will be fixed in priority order decided by our team.
  • We will endeavour to notify you when your bug has been fixed.

Use the weekly delivery clinics (Wednesday, 10:15) with DUX, ADOFOS and FOS teams for help with the dashboard.  Contact Paul Thwaite to attend a clinic.
